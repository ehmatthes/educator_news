Educator News - Features

Educator News aims to be simple and easy to use. Every feature has a clear purpose, and this page will help you understand what the purpose of each new feature is.

Conversations

One of the main goals of Educator News is to enocurage high-level conversation between participants. To maintain a high-level conversation, you need to know when someone has replied to one of your comments. It is particularly important to know if someone has asked you a follow-up question, or answered a question that you posed. The Conversations link allows you to see the replies that other people have made to each of your comments. You need to be logged in to see your conversations.

The Conversations page will be improved slightly, to make it easier to follow:

  • Right now the conversations are in an order based on your highest-level comment on each submission. This will be modifed so that conversations with the most recent replies will be listed first.
